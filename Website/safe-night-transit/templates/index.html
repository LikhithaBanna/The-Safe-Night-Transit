CORE CAPABILITIES

Every feature is engineered with safety as the primary design constraint

🔒

Safe Zone Pickup

Rides can only be requested from verified campus safe zones. If unsafe location detected, system suggests nearest safe pickup point.

🔑

Dual OTP Verification

Students verify their van, drivers verify the student. Two-way identity check prevents unauthorized boarding.

🗺️

Route Deviation Detection

Real-time comparison of planned vs actual route. Any deviation instantly triggers admin alert and camera activation.

🚨

Panic Button + Escalation

One-tap emergency alert to admin and security. Automatically activates van camera and notifies emergency contact.

📡

Smart Camera System

Cameras activate ONLY during emergencies—panic, deviation, or suspicious delays. Full access control and event logging.

🏊

Intelligent Ride Pooling

Nearest-neighbor route optimization pools multiple students safely. Dynamic route updates as new requests come in.

📊

Live Safety Score

Each van carries a Safety Score (0–100) based on route adherence, panic history, and delays. Displayed as Safe / Warning / Risk.

✅

Safe Arrival Confirmation

Students must confirm arrival after drop. If no response within 3 minutes, admin is automatically alerted.

🌡️

Risk Zone Heatmap

Admin panel visualizes high-demand and high-risk pickup zones to proactively position vans and security.

SAFETY IN THREE PHASES

Protection before, during, and after every ride

BEFORE

Pre-Ride Safety Checks

Safe zone validation, van/driver assignment, OTP generation, emergency contact registration, and wait-time monitoring. No ride begins without full identity verification.

DURING

Active Monitoring & Response

Continuous route deviation detection, live safety score, panic button with camera activation, trusted contact alerts, and real-time admin visibility into every active ride.

AFTER

Post-Ride Verification

Mandatory safe arrival confirmation, timeout alerts if student doesn't respond, feedback collection to update driver trust score, and full event audit trail.
